Yeh Privacy Policy batati hai ki Mini eSports ("Platform", "Hum") aapka data kaise collect, use, store aur protect karta hai. Platform use karte hue aap is Policy se agree karte hain.

1. ✅ Skill-Based Game Declaration

Mini eSports — Skill Game, Gambling Nahi

Is platform par host kiye jaane wale sabhi tournaments games of skill hain. Match ka result sirf player ki gaming ability, strategy, aim aur performance par depend karta hai — chance ya luck par nahi.

Yeh platform India ke Supreme Court aur High Court judgments ke under skill-based esports category mein aata hai, jo Haryana, Delhi, Punjab aur other permitted states mein legal hai.

Reference: K.R. Lakshmanan v. State of Tamil Nadu (1996) — Skill games legal hain India mein

2. 🔞 Age Restriction — 18+ Only

Sirf 18 Saal Ya Zyada

Yeh platform strictly 18+ users ke liye hai. Paid tournaments mein sirf 18 saal ya zyada umar ke individuals participate kar sakte hain.

Registration ke waqt aap date of birth verify karte hain. Galat age dena = immediate account ban.

18 se kum umar ke kisi bhi user ka data hum knowingly collect nahi karte. Agar aap kisi minor ke parent hain aur unka account hai, to support@miniesports.in par contact karo — hum data delete kar denge.

3. 🗺️ State Restrictions

Restricted States — Paid Tournaments Allowed Nahi

Yeh states real money gaming ko legally restrict karti hain:

  • Telangana — Telangana Gaming Act, 2017
  • Andhra Pradesh — AP Gaming Act Amendment
  • Tamil Nadu — TN Gaming Amendment

In states ke users ko Platform automatically paid tournaments se block karta hai. Bypass karne ki koshish (VPN etc.) = permanent account ban.

Permitted States (Examples)

Haryana, Delhi, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Karnataka aur baaki states mein participation allowed hai.

4. 🎮 Third-Party Game Disclaimer

⚠️ Free Fire / Garena Se Koi Affiliation Nahi

Mini eSports ek independent tournament organizing platform hai. Hum Free Fire (by Garena) ke official partner, affiliate ya authorized agent nahi hain.

Matches third-party game servers (Free Fire by Garena) par played hote hain. Platform sirf tournament organize karta hai — game server operate nahi karta.

Game crashes, server downtime, in-game issues, ya publisher policy changes ke liye hum responsible nahi hain. Publisher (Garena) ke community tournament guidelines follow ki jaati hain.

6. 🪪 KYC Verification

PAN Card Mandatory — Rs.500+ Withdrawal

IT Rules 2023 ke under, Rs.500 se zyada withdrawal ke liye PAN card verify karna mandatory hai.

  • PAN number securely store hota hai — sirf admin access
  • Aadhaar ka sirf last 4 digits store hota hai — full Aadhaar nahi
  • Ek PAN = sirf ek account
  • Galat KYC dena = permanent ban + legal action
  • KYC verify hone mein 24-48 ghante lagte hain
  • KYC data government requirements ke alawa kisi third party ko share nahi kiya jaata

9. 👤 Account Rules

  • Ek user = sirf ek account — multiple accounts = permanent ban
  • Account non-transferable hai — kisi aur ko dena ya bechna prohibited
  • Account security aapki zimmedari — password share mat karo
  • Hum kisi bhi account ko bina reason bataye suspend kar sakte hain agar fraud suspected ho
  • 6+ months inactivity pe account suspend ho sakta hai

10. 🛡️ Anti-Cheat & Fair Play

Zero Tolerance — Cheating = Permanent Ban

Yeh actions turant permanent ban aur prize forfeit kara sakte hain:

  • Multiple accounts banana
  • Hacking, modding, emulator use (unless allowed by admin)
  • Fake screenshots submit karna
  • Match fixing ya collusion
  • Referral abuse
  • VPN use to bypass state restrictions
  • Bot usage

Cheating ke cases mein legal action bhi ho sakta hai.

11. 💳 Wallet & Payment Policy

  • Deposit: UPI se. Minimum Rs.10.
  • Coins: Virtual in-app currency. Real money mein convert nahi hoti. Non-refundable.
  • Withdrawal: UPI se, 24-48 hours mein. Rs.500+ ke liye KYC mandatory.
  • Bonus: Directly withdraw nahi hoti — sirf tournament entry ke liye.
  • Refund: Entry fee refundable sirf agar platform ne match cancel kiya ho.
  • In-app purchases: Non-refundable (coins, premium features).

12. ⚠️ Platform Liability Limitation

Platform In Cheezoon Ke Liye Responsible Nahi Hai

Server errors, game crashes, internet failure, third-party service issues, force majeure events ke karan koi loss hua to platform liable nahi hai.

Maximum platform liability aapke deposited amount tak hi simit hai.
